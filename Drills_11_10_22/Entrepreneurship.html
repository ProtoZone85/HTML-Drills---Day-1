Entrepreneurship

What is Entrepreneurship? Entrepreneurship is the creation or extraction of economic value.[1][2][3] With this definition, entrepreneurship is viewed as change, generally entailing risk beyond what is normally encountered in starting a business, which may include other values than simply economic ones.

The exploitation of entrepreneurial opportunities may include:

  • Developing a business plan
  • Hiring human resources
  • Acquiring financial and material resources
  • Providing leadership
  • Being responsible for both the venture's success or failure
  • Risk aversion
